    the 50th international congress of the egyption society of cardiology

    dear collegues

    It is a great honor to welcome all of you in the golden jubilee of the 50th cardio egypt 2023, the legendary annual cardiology meeting in cairo Since the establishment of cardio egypt, we strive at making the meeting a special and unique educational cardiology platform. All participants and attendees from egypt and all around the world will have the chance to network and learn sophisticated pearls and new scientific advancements from the latest studies or simply from other colleague’s valuable experience in cardiology practice.
    The organizing committee has prepared a tremendous case-based program and I am confident that all of us will enrich our practice through the the knowledge that is presented here. This year’s celebration of the golden jubilee is unique since it is the fiftieth anniversary of cardio egypt we decided to formulate a unique scientific agenda with different collaborations and endorsements. We are privileged to have eminent international and arab guest speakers that are enriching our program. We also have a program for the juniors physicians with all the new techniques and competitions.
    We will do our best to ensure that all delegates, speakers, and chairpersons will have a worthwhile fruitful meeting, and we are anxious to welcome you the 50th cardio egypt 2023
